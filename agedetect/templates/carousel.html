About Leukemia Disease Detection & Counting Cells

An Automatic Leukemia detection and counting of Red Blood Cells by using Image Processing Technique due it is wide applications in various Manual Blood Cell Counting, Time-Consuming & more analysis problems.

What does Leukemia Disease Detection mean?

Leukemia detection and cell counting through image processing involve the automated analysis of microscopic blood cell images.

What are the model use for Leukemia Detection Hub?

Image processing tools, including OpenCV, numpy, django are employed for tasks such as image enhancement, segmentation, and feature extraction. Deep learning models, specifically convolutional neural networks (CNNs), are utilized for classifying blood cell images and distinguishing between normal and leukemia-affected cells

What is the importance of Leukemia Detection Hub?

Accurate and timely leukemia detection holds immense significance for various real-world applications, including medical diagnostics, treatment planning, and research endeavors. The Leukemia Detection Hub serves as a pivotal tool.
